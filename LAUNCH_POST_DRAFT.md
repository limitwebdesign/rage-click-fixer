# Launch post draft

Users keep clicking. Your UI keeps failing.

I built **Rage Click Fixer** to surface rage clicks, dead interactions, and blocked UI flows in real time.

It can:
- detect repeated click clusters
- flag disabled or misleading targets
- highlight a likely working action
- emit structured browser events for logging
